What am I going to do about it?

Given the problems of digusting dirty rooms, difficulty cleaning for the elderly or young, and fatigue of the general repetitive nature of cleaning, I developed the Vroomba! The Vroomba is a user-friendly, manually-controlled cleaning device that you can operate conveniently from your phone or laptop. It makes cleaning accessible, enjoyable, and manageable, helping users maintain a regular cleaning schedule with ease, and enables exciting RC elements for users. The Vroomba utilizes a tank drive systen with a front vaccum pump to suck in any trash along with two additional fans to aid in the suction action. Additionally, it features a removable trashbin that can be easily emptied out once full.

Technical Details

Feature Description
2 DC Motors Enables drive capabilities
1 DC Vaccum Pump Act as suction valve to pick up trash
